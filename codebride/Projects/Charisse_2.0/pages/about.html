Link

Majora's Mask opens with a scene of Link leaving the land of Hyrule on a personal quest. The opening to the game states that he is searching for his friend, Navi. His trip is interrupted by the Skull Kid.

SkullKid

Under the influence of Majora's Mask, which he stole from the Happy Mask Salesman, the Skull Kid becomes a powerful sorcerer. Using this newfound magical might, he wreaks havoc on the land of Termina, ultimately attempting to destroy it by sending the Moon on a collision course with the land.

Deku King

The Deku King imprisoned a monkey in a small cage. He blames the monkey for the disappearance of his daughter, the Deku Princess. Once Link sneaks in and learns the "Sonata of Awakening" the king takes this as evidence that the monkey was involved in the disappearance of the princess. Once Link returns the princess to the Deku Palace, she yells at the King for imprisoning the monkey, which causes the King to apologize.
